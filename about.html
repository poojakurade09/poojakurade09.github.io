It's me, Pooja - and it's nice to see you here!

I'm a Senior Product Designer originally from India, now based in Berlin. My path here wasn't traditional: I taught myself UX from the ground up, later formalising it with a CUA certification from Human Factors International.

I'm a curious creative at heart, always exploring new ideas and possibilities, balanced with a grounded, practical side that cares about what actually works and what it takes to get there.

Over 8+ years, I've worked across mobile, enterprise, automotive and fintech, bringing both visual craft and structured UX thinking to every project I take on.

What I care about most is building products that are clear, useful, and genuinely easy to live with, ones that hold up in the real world, not just in a mockup.

Outside of work, I'm just as curious, always planning the next trip or trying something new in the kitchen.
